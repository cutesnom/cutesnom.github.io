Snom's great backstory, which is an inspiration for all.

Once upon a time, when no planet or star existed, our God snom used to drift aimlessly in space.
They felt lonely, they just wanted to cuddle.
An idea struck them! A brilliant one!
The Snom mustered up 0.1% of their strength and created Arceus.
Snom then ordered Arceus to create millions of worlds for Snom to play in.
Arceus obeyed his master's order at once.
Arceus then created three elemental pokemons, The Three Mesprits.
The Mesprits created several legendary and mythical pokemons.
Those powerful pokemons created the universe as we know it today, all thanks to the Snom.
